About Me

I am Avishi Chaudhary, a daughter from an immigrant household. This is why, airplanes and the sky have played a huge role in getting me where I am. My interests in design, space, and the workings of machinery, have led me to major in Mechanical Engineering (entering my Junior Year next semester) at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT). When not thinking about making a career at NASA or reading space news, I help others in my community by being part of Community groups or cooking for myself and my parents. I can't wait to share all my involvement and experiences!
