Hello, welcome aboard my flight! I am Avishi Chaudhary, a daughter of an immigrant household. Airplanes and the sky have played a huge role in getting me where I am, which inspired the design of this website. My parents and I moved to the US in December 2019 from Rajasthan, India, and we all recently became US citizens!

My interests in design, space, and the workings of machinery have led me to major in Mechanical Engineering at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, where I will enter my Junior Year next semester. A few things to know about me: I love helping people and making an impact in whatever way possible while enjoying designing, creating, and staying close to my creative side.

When I’m not contemplating a career at NASA, reading space news, or figuring out what to learn next, I help others in my community by participating in community groups, cooking for myself and my parents, or assisting freshmen with their college move-in. I can’t wait to share all my involvement and experiences!
